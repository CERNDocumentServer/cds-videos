Help

In order to help you in the most efficient way it is important to distinguish between:

  • the material contained in CERN Document Server:

    For issues regarding the content of CDS you should refer to the service in charge of the material (publication, image, video, etc.) according to references you find in the page (including the name of the collections of records) or contact the author(s)

  • the CERN Document Server application itself (functions, issues, etc.):

    For issues about using the application itself, please:

    1. firstly check the FAQ page
    2. if that does not help then check the Tutorial
    3. you can also contact the CDS team via the CERN Service portal or by email
