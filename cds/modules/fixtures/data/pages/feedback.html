Feedback

Feedback about CDS content (information about the record or the record itself) should be adressed to the reference person/service (typically the author, the experiment, etc.). For feedback regarding the content of librarian collections (books, periodicals, etc.), please contact the Library.

For feedback about the CDS application itself (functions, options, issues, aspect, etc.), please contact the CDS Service after checking the Frequently Asked Questions - FAQ and the Tutorial.
